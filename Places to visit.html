Places to Visit in Jabalpur

  • Bhedaghat Marble Rocks

    When river Narmada crosses between the mountains of white marble it seems lik a scene straight out of a movie, Its beauty is even tempting for so many movie makers thats why every year so many movies and songs were filmed here such as Ashoka, Dunki, Mohan jodaro etc. .

  • Dhuandhar Falls

    When the water of river narmada falls from the height of 30 meters the pressure and force of that make it looks milky white and giving its name Dhuandhar meaning a stream of milk, it also produce mist of water.

  • Balancing Rock

    It is a natural miracle that a massive rock of many tons is balanced on another rock in getting support of very less space, this natural wonder defy gravity in all sense.

  • Madan Mahal Fort

    A historical fort built by the Gond ruler Madan Shah in 1116 CE. This fort is a symbol of pride, it reminds the people of jabalpur about the bravery of Queen Rani Durgawati.
