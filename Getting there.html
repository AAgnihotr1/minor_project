Getting to Jabalpur

Jabalpur is well-connected to major cities in India. Here are the primary modes of transportation:

By Air

Jabalpur is connected to nearly all major cities in the country by its Airport, Dumna Airport built in 1960s.

By Train

Jabalpur Junction (JBP) is a major railway station and the Zonal headquaters of west central railways, it was built in 1867 and since then it is providing connectivity towards Mumbai,Delhi,Howrah,Chennai and all major cities of India. Jabalpur Junction handles nearly 190 to 200 trains per day and thousands of passangers making it a grade a railway station of Indian railways.

By Road

There are multiple national and state highways are crossed from Jabalpur such as NH 44 previously known as NH 7, MP SH 22 and many more promenent roads.
