Local Cuisine of Jabalpur

As Madhya Pradesh is the heart of India and it is also the melting pot of different flavours, so being a part of Madhya Pradesh Jabalpur is no different in terms of food, Jabalpur has a rich culinary herritage and love for flavours. Here are some must-try local specialties:

  • Poha

    Poha and Jalebi it is like the permanent breakfast of locals in Jabalpur as you can even find some venders strated selling it even 4 in the morning.

  • Bhutte Ka Kees

    A unique dish made from grated corn, milk, and spices.

  • Dal bati bharta

    Dal bati and bharta absolute classic for the residents of Jabalpur, If you ask any local about what you should try they will definitely recommend you this delicacy.

  • Khoya Jalebi

    Jalebi made from khoya or mawa, was invented by Harprasad Badkul, in the year 1889, in Jabalpur. It is a very famous dish in Jabalpur as it is originated herer so the locals have a different kind of love for this sweet.

Don't forget to try the local street food and visit some of the famous restaurants in Jabalpur to experience the authentic flavors of the region.
